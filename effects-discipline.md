# Effects Discipline

## Purpose

Use shadows, gradients, and visual effects to clarify depth and emphasis without clutter.

## Repeatable Practice

- Use shadows to communicate elevation, layering, or interactivity.
- Keep one consistent light source.
- Use gradients sparingly for depth, emphasis, or primary calls to action.
- Keep gradients smooth and legible.
- Avoid effects that fight content readability.
- Use highlights and texture only when they support focus.

## Development Checklist

- Shadows are tokenized or consistently defined.
- Hover and focus effects clarify interactivity.
- Gradients do not reduce text contrast.
- Effects are not required to understand the UI.
