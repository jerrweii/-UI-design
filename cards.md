# Cards

## Purpose

Use cards to package related information and actions in a compact, scannable format.

## Repeatable Practice

- Keep card content concise.
- Use clear hierarchy: image or icon, title, metadata, description, action.
- Use spacing and typography to guide reading order.
- Avoid stuffing too much content into one card.
- Make card CTAs clear, tappable, and visually distinct.
- Keep card dimensions stable across repeated grids.
- Use decorative elements in moderation.

## Development Checklist

- Cards align in grids and lists.
- Long titles and descriptions have defined behavior.
- CTAs are reachable on touch devices.
- Card spacing is consistent.
- Repeated card sets remain comparable.
