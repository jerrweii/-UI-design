# Pricing UX

## Purpose

Design pricing sections that communicate value, build trust, and make comparison easy.

## Repeatable Practice

- Communicate product value before focusing on price.
- Highlight the recommended plan intentionally.
- Use social proof when available.
- Add trust signals such as secure payment, brand associations, or testimonials.
- Keep important comparison information visible in long pricing tables.
- Use discounts and scarcity carefully; false urgency damages trust.
- Reduce friction with trials, guarantees, or clear cancellation terms when appropriate.

## Development Checklist

- Plans are easy to compare.
- The recommended plan is visually distinct but not manipulative.
- Feature differences are clear.
- Trust and risk-reduction cues are close to the purchase action.
- Pricing labels and billing intervals are unambiguous.
